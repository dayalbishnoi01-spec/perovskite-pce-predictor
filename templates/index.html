About Perovskite Solar Cells

Perovskite solar cells follow the ABX₃ crystal structure — A is an organic/inorganic cation (MA, FA, Cs), B is a metal (Pb, Sn), and X is a halide (I, Br, Cl). Their efficiency has risen from 3.8% in 2009 to over 26% today.

This tool uses a Random Forest model trained on 13,361 real experimental records from the NOMAD database to predict PCE from composition and fabrication parameters — enabling virtual screening before costly lab work.
